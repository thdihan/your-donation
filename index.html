Our Goals

We have specific goals to achieve with your valuable donations. These are picked from most commons issue around the country.

Food for a family

We will try to ensure food for every family. Food is a basic necessity of a human. We will provide good and healthy food 3 times to all the family

Shelter for a family

We are working to provide shelter to a family and help them to maintain a proper lifestyle. This is an ongoing process and we will achieve 5000 within a month.

Education for every child.

We are going to ensure quality ensure for every child. We have established schools for all levels so that these childrens can achieve primary to secondary level education from their home.
